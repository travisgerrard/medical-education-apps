# Common Symptoms of Anxiety

Anxiety affects both your mind and your body.

## Emotional Symptoms
- **Excessive Worry**: Worrying about everyday events or problems.
- **Restlessness**: Feeling keyed up or on edge.
- **Irritability**: Becoming easily annoyed or frustrated.
- **Dread**: A persistent feeling that something bad is about to happen.

## Physical Symptoms
- **Muscle Tension**: Tightness in your neck, shoulders, or back.
- **Fatigue**: Feeling tired even after resting.
- **Sleep Problems**: Trouble falling or staying asleep.
- **Stomach Issues**: Nausea or digestive problems.

>
> Physical symptoms are real. Treating the anxiety often resolves the physical discomfort.
