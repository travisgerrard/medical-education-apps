#Introduction

If you do fall, knowing what to do can prevent injury and complications. This final section prepares you to respond effectively and learn from the experience.

## Why This Section Matters

**After a fall:**
- Immediate response matters
- Getting help quickly is critical
- Medical evaluation important even if you feel fine
- Each fall is a chance to prevent the next one

## What This Section Covers

1. When to call 911
2. How to get up safely if you can
3. When to call your doctor
4. What to watch for after a fall
5. Preventing the next fall
