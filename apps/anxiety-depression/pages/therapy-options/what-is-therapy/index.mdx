# What Therapy Is and How It Helps

Therapy involves talking with a trained professional to identify and change troubling emotions, thoughts, and behaviors.

## How It Helps
- **Identify Triggers**: Learn what situations or thoughts set off your symptoms.
- **Change Patterns**: Replace negative thought patterns with healthier ones.
- **Learn Skills**: Develop coping mechanisms for stress and difficult emotions.
- **Process Emotions**: Work through past trauma or grief in a safe environment.

## It's Collaborative
Therapy is a partnership. You and your therapist work together to set goals and track progress.

> [!TIP]
> You get out of therapy what you put into it. Practicing skills between sessions is key.
