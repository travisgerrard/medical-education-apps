# Introduction

Knowing when to reach out for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. In this section, we'll discuss when to see a professional and how to handle urgent situations.

## Key Topics
- Recognizing when symptoms require professional care
- Identifying warning signs of a crisis
- Resources for immediate help
- Supporting others in need

>
> If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.
