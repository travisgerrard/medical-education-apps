# Introduction

Talk therapy (psychotherapy) is a cornerstone of treatment for anxiety and depression. It provides a safe space to explore your thoughts and learn new skills.

## What We'll Cover
- How therapy works
- Different types of therapy (CBT, ACT)
- What to expect from the process
- Finding the right therapist for you

> [!NOTE]
> Therapy is not just "venting." It is an active process of learning and growth.
