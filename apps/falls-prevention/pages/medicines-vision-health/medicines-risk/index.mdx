# Medicines That Increase Fall Risk

This lesson builds on the earlier detailed medication lesson to reinforce key points about managing fall risk from medications.

## Key Medication Categories

Review the comprehensive medication lesson in Section 2 "Understanding Risk" for detailed information on:
- Sedatives and sleep medications
- Blood pressure medications
- Antidepressants
- Opioid pain medications
- Anti-anxiety medications

## Critical Actions

**Annual medication review:**
- Bring ALL medications (including OTC) to doctor appointments
- Ask specifically about fall risk
- Request safer alternatives
- Discuss dose reductions

**Work with pharmacist:**
- Free medication therapy management
- Identify drug interactions
- Suggest timing strategies

**Monitor yourself:**
- Note any new dizziness after medication changes
- Report balance problems promptly
- Keep updated medication list

## Remember

Never stop medications without consulting your doctor. The goal is to use the safest options at the lowest effective doses through partnership with your healthcare team.
