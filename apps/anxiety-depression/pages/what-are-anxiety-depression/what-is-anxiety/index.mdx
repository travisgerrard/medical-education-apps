# What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is more than just feeling stressed or worried. It is a persistent feeling of fear or dread that can interfere with your daily life.

## Normal Worry vs. Anxiety Disorder

Everyone feels anxious sometimes. It's a normal reaction to stress. But for people with anxiety disorders, the fear is not temporary and can be overwhelming.

### Common Signs
- Feeling restless or on edge
- Being easily fatigued
- Difficulty concentrating
- Irritability
- Muscle tension
- Sleep problems

## Types of Anxiety
- **Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)**: Excessive worry about everyday things.
- **Panic Disorder**: Sudden periods of intense fear (panic attacks).
- **Social Anxiety Disorder**: Intense fear of social situations.

>
> Anxiety is highly treatable. You don't have to live with constant worry.
