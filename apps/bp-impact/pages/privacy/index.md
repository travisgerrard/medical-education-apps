# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: November 19, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to BP Impact ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by BP Impact.

## Information Collection and Use

BP Impact is designed to help you track and manage your blood pressure.

**We do not collect any personal data.** All data entered into the app, including blood pressure readings and logs, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers.

## Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend keeping your device secure to protect your health information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
