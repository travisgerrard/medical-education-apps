# Common Symptoms of Depression

Depression is more than just a low mood. It impacts your entire body.

## Emotional Symptoms
- **Persistent Sadness**: Feeling empty or "down" most of the day.
- **Loss of Interest**: No longer enjoying hobbies or time with friends.
- **Guilt or Worthlessness**: Feeling like a burden to others.
- **Hopelessness**: Believing that things will never get better.

## Physical Symptoms
- **Changes in Appetite**: Significant weight loss or gain.
- **Sleep Changes**: Insomnia or sleeping too much (hypersomnia).
- **Fatigue**: Loss of energy or feeling slowed down.
- **Aches and Pains**: Unexplained headaches or body aches.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you have thoughts of death or suicide, please seek help immediately. Call or text 988.
