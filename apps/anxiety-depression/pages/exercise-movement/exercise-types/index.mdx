# Which Kinds of Exercise Help Most for Depression

Different types of exercise offer different benefits.

## Aerobic Exercise
**Examples**: Walking, running, swimming, cycling
**Benefits**:
- Increases endorphins (natural mood boosters)
- Improves sleep
- Reduces anxiety

**Recommendation**: 30 minutes, 3-5 times per week

## Strength Training
**Examples**: Weightlifting, resistance bands, bodyweight exercises
**Benefits**:
- Builds confidence and self-efficacy
- Improves body image
- Releases mood-boosting hormones

**Recommendation**: 2-3 times per week

## Yoga and Mind-Body Exercise
**Benefits**:
- Combines physical movement with mindfulness
- Reduces stress and muscle tension
- Improves mind-body connection

> [!TIP]
> The best exercise is the one you'll actually do. Choose something you enjoy!
