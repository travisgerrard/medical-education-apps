# Introduction

Medications can be an effective part of treatment for anxiety and depression. This section explains how they work and what to expect.

## What We'll Cover
- How antidepressants affect brain chemistry
- Common medication types (SSRIs, SNRIs)
- Side effects and safety
- Starting and stopping medications safely

> [!NOTE]
> Medication is one tool among many. It works best when combined with therapy and lifestyle changes.
