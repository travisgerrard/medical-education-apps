# Who Benefits Most from Medication

Medication is most helpful for moderate to severe depression or anxiety.

## Good Candidates for Medication
- **Moderate to Severe Symptoms**: PHQ-9 score of 10 or higher, GAD-7 score of 10 or higher
- **Functional Impairment**: Symptoms interfere with work, relationships, or daily activities
- **Previous Success**: Medication helped you in the past
- **Preference**: You prefer medication or want faster relief while waiting for therapy to work

## When Medication May Not Be First Choice
- **Mild Symptoms**: May respond well to therapy alone or lifestyle changes
- **Preference for Non-Medication**: You prefer to try therapy or exercise first
- **Medical Contraindications**: Certain health conditions or other medications make antidepressants risky

> [!TIP]
> There's no "right" answer. Discuss your preferences and concerns openly with your doctor.
