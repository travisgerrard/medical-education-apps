# Vision and Hearing

Good vision and hearing are essential for balance and fall prevention. Regular check-ups and proper correction can significantly reduce fall risk.

## Vision and Falls

**Why vision matters:**
- Sees obstacles and hazards
- Judges distances and depth
- Detects changes in surface
- Helps brain understand body position

**Statistics**: Vision problems **double** fall risk.

## Common Vision Problems

### Cataracts
- Cloudy lens
- Blurry vision
- Difficulty with glare
- Poor night vision
- **Surgery reduces fall risk by 30%**

### Glaucoma
- Peripheral vision loss
- Can't see obstacles to side
- Often no symptoms until advanced
- **Regular screening critical** (annual eye exams)

### Macular Degeneration
- Central vision loss
- Difficulty seeing details
- Poor depth perception
- Can't see step edges clearly

### Diabetic Retinopathy
- From diabetes
- Vision changes
- Can lead to blindness
- **Tight blood sugar control prevents**

## Vision Care

**Annual eye exams essential for those over 60**

**Update prescriptions:**
- Changes can be gradual (you adapt without realizing)
- Outdated glasses increase fall risk
- Check prescription annually

**Bifocals and multifocals:**
- Can distort vision on stairs
- Consider separate reading/distance glasses
- Single-vision distance glasses for walking outdoors

**Lighting:**
- Aging eyes need 2-3x more light
- Eliminate glare
- Use task lighting

**After eye procedures:**
- Be extra careful during adjustment period
- Use railings
- Avoid stairs if vision blurry

## Hearing and Balance

**The connection:**
- Inner ear controls balance
- Hearing loss may indicate balance system problems
- Can't hear environmental cues
- May not hear someone warning about hazard

**Hearing loss increases fall risk by 30-140%** (depending on severity).

### Common Causes
- Age-related hearing loss
- Ear infections
- Earwax buildup (simple fix!)
- Meniere's disease
- Inner ear disorders

## Vestibular Disorders

**Inner ear problems:**
- BPPV (benign positional vertigo)
- Vestibular neuritis
- Labyrinth itis

**Symptoms:**
- Room spinning
- Severe dizziness
- Nausea
- Balance problems

**Treatment:**
- Vestibular rehabilitation therapy
- Epley maneuver for BPPV
- Medications
- Balance retraining

## Hearing Aids

**Benefits beyond hearing:**
- May improve balance
- Better environmental awareness
- Reduces isolation
- Improves safety

**Getting help:**
- Audiologist evaluation
- Modern aids are discreet
- Insurance may cover (check benefits)
- Start with one ear if cost prohibitive

## Regular Screenings

**Vision:**
- Age 60+: Annual comprehensive eye exam
- With diabetes: Annual dilated exam
- If vision changes: Immediate exam

**Hearing:**
- Baseline at age 50
- Every 3 years if normal
- Annually if problems noted

## When to seek help immediately

**Vision:**
- Sudden vision loss
- Flashes of light
- New floaters
- Eye pain
- Double vision

**Hearing/Balance:**
- Sudden hearing loss
- Severe dizziness
- Room spinning
- Unsteady gait
- Falls
