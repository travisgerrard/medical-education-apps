# Introduction

![Doctor explaining medication options](/images/medication_doctor.png)

Medications can be an important part of treatment for many people with anxiety and depression. They work by balancing chemicals in your brain that affect mood and emotions.

## What We'll Cover
- How antidepressants affect brain chemistry
- Common medication types (SSRIs, SNRIs)
- Side effects and safety
- Starting and stopping medications safely

>
> Medication is one tool among many. It works best when combined with therapy and lifestyle changes.
