# Introduction

![Person practicing mindfulness](/images/mindfulness_calm.png)

While therapy and medication are powerful tools, the skills you practice every day can make a huge difference in your recovery. These are practical techniques you can use anytime, anywhere.

## What We'll Cover
- Breathing and grounding techniques
- Managing worry and negative thoughts
- Behavioral activation
- Sleep and light exposure
- Substances and mood

>
> These skills work best when practiced regularly, not just during a crisis.
