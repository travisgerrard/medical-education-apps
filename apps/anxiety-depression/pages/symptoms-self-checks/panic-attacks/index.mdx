# Panic Attacks

A panic attack is a sudden episode of intense fear that triggers severe physical reactions when there is no real danger or apparent cause.

## What It Feels Like
- **Racing Heart**: Palpitations or pounding heart.
- **Shortness of Breath**: Feeling like you can't get enough air.
- **Chest Pain**: Tightness or discomfort in the chest.
- **Dizziness**: Feeling lightheaded or faint.
- **Sweating or Chills**: Sudden temperature changes.
- **Fear of Dying**: A sense of impending doom.

## It Will Pass
Panic attacks are frightening, but they are not dangerous. They typically peak within minutes and then subside.

> [!TIP]
> Grounding techniques, like deep breathing, can help you ride out a panic attack.
