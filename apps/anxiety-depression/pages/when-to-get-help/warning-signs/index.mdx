# Warning Signs That Need Urgent Help

Some symptoms require immediate attention.

## Red Flags
- **Thoughts of Suicide**: Thinking about hurting or killing yourself.
- **Self-Harm**: Engaging in behaviors that cause physical injury.
- **Psychosis**: Hearing voices or seeing things that aren't there.
- **Severe Hopelessness**: Feeling like there is no way out.
- **Inability to Function**: Not eating, drinking, or bathing.

## What to Do
- **Call 988**: The Suicide & Crisis Lifeline is available 24/7.
- **Go to the ER**: If you are in immediate danger.
- **Call a Friend**: Ask someone to stay with you until you get help.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Take these signs seriously. Immediate help is available.
