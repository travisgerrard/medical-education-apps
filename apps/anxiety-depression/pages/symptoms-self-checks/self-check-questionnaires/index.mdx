# Self Check Questionnaires

Clinicians use standardized tools to screen for anxiety and depression and track progress.

## PHQ-9 (Depression)
The Patient Health Questionnaire-9 asks about the frequency of depressive symptoms over the last 2 weeks.
- **Scores**:
  - 5-9: Mild
  - 10-14: Moderate
  - 15-19: Moderately Severe
  - 20+: Severe

## GAD-7 (Anxiety)
The Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7 asks about anxiety symptoms over the last 2 weeks.
- **Scores**:
  - 5-9: Mild
  - 10-14: Moderate
  - 15+: Severe

## What Scores Mean
These scores help your care team understand the severity of your symptoms and decide on the best treatment. They are also used to measure improvement over time.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A high score suggests you may benefit from treatment, but only a clinician can provide a diagnosis.
