# Introduction
![Abstract puzzle pieces coming together](/images/shared_decision.png)

Shared decision making is a collaborative process where you and your healthcare provider work together to make health care decisions that are best for you. It combines the clinician's expertise with your personal values and preferences.

Deciding whether to take medication is a personal choice. This section helps you weigh the benefits and risks and make an informed decision with your doctor.

## What We'll Cover
- Who benefits most from medication
- Comparing treatment options
- What to expect in the first weeks
- When to adjust your treatment
- Planning for long-term use

>
> The best treatment plan is one that fits your values, preferences, and lifestyle.
